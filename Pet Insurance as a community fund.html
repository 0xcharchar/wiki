Pet Insurance DAO

CALL IT PETS.INSURANCE FOR THE MEMES

Goals

  • visible pool of funds for transparency
  • clear claim rulings
  • community governance with vote delegation:
    • probably a governance token like Gitcoin
  • transparent raising/lowering of monthly fees:
    • fees are based on size of fund with the goal of lowering fees over time
    • use DeFi to increase fund (with goal of lowering fees)
  • pay professionals to review claims
  • equal payments for all participants

"Business Case"

This isn't a business, its a community fund, but we should know how much insurance companies make to understand if we can do better.
