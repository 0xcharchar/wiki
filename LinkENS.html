L2 Notes

L2 subdomains are pretty straightforward but the biggest gap is a lack of support from clients. We would want to have support in Metamask, mobile wallets, and Cloudflare's CDN thing before going down this path. We could do L1 subdomains with L2 record resolvers. This would require the user to do two transactions from their wallet, one on L1 and one on L2. We could skip the L2 transaction by covering the gas cost (should be significantly lower anyways).
